Simon Game!

HOW TO PLAY

  1. When you press begin, a sequence of length 1 will play.
  2. Your job is to repeat that sequence by clicking on the same colors that flash.
  3. Each time you successfully complete a sequence, the same sequence will play, but one more button will play.
  4. Reach a streak of 20 to win!
  5. Note: If you unsuccessfully copy the sequence, it will play again. If strict mode is on, you will have to start from the beginning.

TIPS

  • It may be easier to follow the sounds than the colors.
  • If you are following the colors, it may be easier to keep track of the first letter of each color in your head.
  • This game may help improve your short-term memory
